------------
Amazon Polly
------------

Step 1: Open your Amazon Management console
Step 2: Open Amazon Polly dashboard
    1. Click `Try Polly` for new Users
Step 3: Type in your input text in the field provided
Step 4: Check the output audio by clicking `Listen` button on top right
Step 5: Once satisfied, click `Download` button to download the audio file.

------------
Upload to S3
------------

Step 1: Open the S3 console
Step 2: Click 'Create Bucket' Button
Step 3: Configure the New Bucket
    1. Select the bucket type as 'General Purpose'
    2. Provide a Bucket Name to your Bucket. The bucket name should be globally unique
    3. Click on `Create Bucket` button on the bottom right
Step 4: Once created, upload your audio file downloaded into the S3 bucket by dragging the file onto it
